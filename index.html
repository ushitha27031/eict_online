ℹ️ How EICT Online Works

Everything about the program, your coins, the secret codes, and what rewards actually mean.

🛰️ What is EICT Online?

EICT Online is a 50-day mission-style revision program built to get you fully prepared for your ICT final exam. Instead of one big pile of revision, the syllabus is broken into bite-sized "missions" — each one a short paper covering a specific topic. You complete missions one at a time, in order, and earn 🪙 coins for every step you take.

🧭 How a mission works

  • Step 1 — Submit: Open the mission paper, answer it, then paste a Google Drive link to your answer sheet. This instantly unlocks the next mission so you're never stuck waiting, and earns you coins right away.
  • Step 2 — Watch, then unlock: Once you've submitted, you can open the discussion video. Near the end of it, your teacher reveals a secret code just for that mission. You must type that code into the app to unlock the marking scheme and your marked answer sheet — there's no way to see either one without actually watching the discussion. Getting the code right also earns you bonus coins.
  • Step 3 — Your result: Your teacher reviews your submission and enters a score. Score-based bonus coins are added once that happens — there's no rush, you've already moved on to the next mission.

🪙 What are coins, really?

Coins are your in-app currency — you earn them for every mission action (submitting, unlocking the marking scheme with the right code, scoring well, keeping a streak alive). They do two things:

  • They set your Rank (from 🟢 Agent all the way up to 🏆 ICT Legend) and your position on the Coins leaderboard — this is based on your total coins earned, and spending coins never lowers it.
  • They're spendable in the 🎬 Video Store to buy extra lesson videos your teacher uploads. Your spendable balance ("Wallet") goes down when you buy something, but your all-time earned total (and your rank) stays exactly the same.

🔥 Streaks

A "streak" is the number of missions you've completed back-to-back without a gap. Hit a 3-mission or 5-mission streak and you get a coin bonus automatically.

🏅 Ranking Papers

After every couple of missions, a Ranking Paper appears — a combined paper covering everything so far. These are worth more coins and help you (and your teacher) see exactly where you stand before moving further. They never block your mission progress, so you can keep going even if one is still being graded.

📚 Study Plans

There are structured study plans covering the full syllabus. You don't get them all at once — each plan unlocks automatically as you complete the missions tied to it, so you're always studying material that matches where you are in the program.

🎬 Video Store

Your teacher can upload extra lesson videos to the store, each with its own coin price. If you've earned enough coins, you can buy one straight away — once purchased, it's yours to re-watch anytime from the Video Store page.

🎁 What are Milestone Rewards for?

Milestone Rewards are separate from coins — they're unlocked purely by how many missions you've completed, not by how many coins you have or spend. They're extra academic material and recognition: bonus revision papers, a prediction paper for the final, an official completion certificate, and a permanent spot in the Hall of Fame. They exist to keep you motivated to finish — the real reward is walking into your final exam fully prepared.

🏆 Two leaderboards

The Leaderboard page has two views: Total Marks, ranked by the actual marks your teacher has given you across every graded paper (the one that matters academically), and Coins Earned, ranked by how consistently you've been showing up and completing steps. Switch between them anytime.
